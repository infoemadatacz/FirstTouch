What changes for the partnership

Three outcomes a managing partner can take to the partnership meeting.

Not a technology pitch. Three clear answers to the questions your partnership will ask: where does client data go, how does this know our practice, and what happens when the SRA comes calling. Each one measured in hours, risk, and pounds.

Client data never leaves your infrastructure. Ever.

Client confidentiality is not a preference — it is a professional obligation. Sending client matter data to a public AI service without explicit consent and lawful basis is a potential breach of both your SRA obligations and UK GDPR. FirstTouch eliminates that risk entirely.

The system runs on servers you control — on-premise or in a private cloud your firm owns. Your matter files, client correspondence, fee arrangements, and commercial data do not pass through our infrastructure. We configure it. You own it. Your clients' privilege is preserved.

No matter data ever processed by third-party AI infrastructure
Matter-level access controls — fee-earners see only their matters
Your data is never used to train any model
⚖️

AI that knows your matter types, your precedents, your firm.

A generic AI assistant doesn't know your standard NDA, your preferred liability cap position, or which clauses your senior partner always flags. FirstTouch is configured against your own documents — your precedent library, your matter files, your know-how.

A fee earner asks "what's our standard position on IP ownership in contractor agreements?" and gets the answer from your own precedents — not a generic template from the internet. That's the difference between a tool that saves time and one that creates rework.

Connected to your document management system and precedent library
Matter summaries pulled from your own file notes in seconds
First drafts of standard letters using your firm's own templates
📋

A complete audit trail for every action. Ready for any review.

The SRA has been clear: firms using AI must maintain adequate supervision, understand how tools process data, and be able to demonstrate it. "I didn't know where the data was going" will not satisfy a professional conduct review.

FirstTouch logs every query, every document accessed, every AI-assisted draft produced — timestamped, attributed to the fee earner, and stored on your infrastructure. When your next PII review comes around, or a client raises a question about how their matter was handled, the record is there.
