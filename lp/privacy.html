2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Contact Form

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Business email address
  • Company name (optional)
  • Message content

Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR — processing necessary to take pre-contractual steps at your request; and/or Art. 6(1)(f) — our legitimate interest in responding to business inquiries.

2.2 Analytics

We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-first analytics platform that does not use cookies, does not collect personal identifiers, and stores aggregate data only. No cookie consent banner is required under EU law for this tool.

2.3 Server Logs

Cloudflare Pages, our hosting infrastructure, automatically logs IP addresses and HTTP request metadata for security, abuse prevention, and performance purposes. These logs are not used for marketing and are subject to Cloudflare's own retention policies (typically 30 days).

3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected personal data solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or service request
  • Send product updates if you have explicitly opted in
  • Improve our website (aggregate, anonymised analytics only)
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority. In the United Kingdom: Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) — ico.org.uk. If you are based in another EU member state, contact the supervisory authority of your country of residence.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures (TOMs) as required by Article 32 GDPR, including:

  • TLS 1.2+ / HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Cloudflare WAF and DDoS protection layer
  • Access controls: data accessible only to authorised personnel
  • No storage of contact data in publicly accessible systems

No method of internet transmission is 100% secure. In the event of a personal data breach, we will notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours (Article 33 GDPR) and affected data subjects where required by Article 34 GDPR.

8. Children's Data

First Touch is a B2B service for business professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 16 years of age. If you believe a child has submitted data to us, please contact us immediately at james@firsttouch.app and we will delete it promptly.

The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes acknowledgement of the updated policy.
