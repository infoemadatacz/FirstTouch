CDM and HSE compliance — tracked across every live site simultaneously.

Running five sites means five sets of CDM obligations, five permit renewal schedules, five inspection records. Your commercial team can't hold all of that in their heads. And when a principal contractor or the HSE asks a compliance question, "let me check and come back to you" is not the answer you want to give.

FirstTouch tracks compliance status across all your active projects, flags expiry dates before they become problems, and generates the documentation your F10, pre-construction information, and health and safety files require. No manual spreadsheet. No chasing site managers for updates.

Permit expiry tracking flagged automatically across all sites
CDM Phase 2 documentation generated from your project data
Audit trail of every compliance action — timestamped and retrievable

Tender packs built from your own project history, not from scratch.

Your estimating team spends days on every significant tender — pulling together company statements, insurance certificates, CHAS accreditations, project references, and method statements. Much of this is the same documentation repurposed. The time cost is real and it compounds across every bid.

FirstTouch knows your full project library. It assembles the relevant sections from past tenders that match the new brief, surfaces the right project references, and drafts the operational methodology sections your team can then review and refine. Bid preparation time drops from days to hours.
