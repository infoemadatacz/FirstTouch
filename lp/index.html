FAQ

Is this just a ChatGPT wrapper?

No. It is a model-agnostic execution layer that can use different models and tools, then take real actions.

Can it really handle coding, writing, planning, and reporting in one place?

Yes. The assistant can support strategic writing, planning, coding workflows, meeting-to-action loops, and recurring KPI reporting.

Do we need to replace CRM, ERP, calendar, or support systems?

No. First Touch sits above your current stack and orchestrates actions through integrations.

How is compliance handled for autonomous actions?

Through RBAC, policy-based autonomy thresholds, human escalation controls, and complete action trails.

Who should own this internally?

Usually COO or RevOps as business owner, partnered with IT/Security and CEO sponsorship.
