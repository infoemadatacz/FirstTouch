FAQ

Questions we get asked
before every strategy call.

Will it actually sound like me?

Yes. During onboarding, we analyze your existing content, speaking patterns, vocabulary, and tone. The AI builds a voice model specific to you. Every post goes through your Telegram for approval — if something doesn't sound right, you edit it, and the AI learns from the correction. After 2-3 weeks, most clients approve 90%+ of posts without changes.

How much time do I actually need to spend?

Under 5 minutes per day. You get a morning Telegram message with today's posts. Tap approve, or send a quick voice message with feedback. That's it. Most clients spend less than 20 minutes per week total.

What if I want to write a post myself?

Great — just send it via Telegram. The system schedules it alongside AI-generated content. Your original posts and AI posts alternate naturally. You can also dictate ideas as voice messages and the AI will turn them into polished posts.

Which platforms do you support?

LinkedIn, X (Twitter), and Instagram. Each platform gets content adapted to its format and algorithm — LinkedIn gets long-form thought leadership, X gets punchy takes and threads, Instagram gets visual carousels and stories. You choose which platforms you want active.

How is this different from hiring a ghostwriter?

Three ways: consistency, speed, and cost. A ghostwriter posts when they have time. Our AI posts 3-4 times per week, every week, without fail. New content is generated in minutes, not days. And it costs a fraction of a senior ghostwriter (who charges €2,000-5,000/month). Plus, the AI never goes on vacation.

What happens during the first week?

Day 1: 30-minute onboarding call — we learn your voice, goals, and audience. Day 2-3: We configure the system, calibrate your AI voice model, and set up your content pillars. Day 4-5: First batch of posts generated and sent to your Telegram for review. Day 6-7: First posts go live. By day 7, you have a fully operational content engine.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly billing, cancel anytime with 30 days notice. No lock-in contracts. We believe the results keep you, not a contract. Your content, your accounts — everything stays yours.

Is my data safe? Where does it go?

Your data stays in EU-hosted infrastructure (Supabase on AWS eu-central). We never share your content, voice model, or personal information with third parties. Platform API tokens are encrypted at rest. You can request full data export or deletion at any time.
