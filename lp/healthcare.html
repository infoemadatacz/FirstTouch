Why it works for healthcare

Built for practices where data
protection is non-negotiable.

Three things that matter most to practice owners and managers — and how FirstTouch delivers on all three.

GDPR and CQC compliance — built in from day one.

Healthcare data sits under the tightest regulatory scrutiny in any sector. Your practice needs a watertight audit trail for every patient interaction the AI touches — and it needs to be ready for a CQC inspection without a last-minute scramble.

FirstTouch is configured to your specific compliance obligations. Every AI-assisted action is logged, timestamped, and attributed. Your DPO gets the documentation they need. Your CQC inspection prep takes hours, not weeks.

Full audit log of every AI-assisted task — ready for CQC or ICO review
GDPR Article 32 controls built into the system architecture
Data Processing Agreement (DPA) provided as standard

AI that knows your protocols — not generic medical advice.

A generic AI chatbot doesn't know how your triage works, what your referral pathway looks like, or what consent wording your practice uses. It gives generic answers that your staff can't act on.

FirstTouch is trained on your own documentation — your clinical protocols, your internal policies, your letter templates. When a receptionist asks how to handle an urgent query, the answer reflects your practice, not a textbook.

Trained on your clinical protocols, SOPs, and letter templates
Answers reflect your practice's actual pathways — not generic guidance
Update your protocols and the AI updates automatically

No patient data ever leaves your practice. Not one byte.

Sending patient data to a third-party AI cloud service — even anonymised — creates real GDPR and ICO exposure. Your patients trusted you with their information. That trust shouldn't depend on the privacy policy of a US software company.

FirstTouch runs entirely on your own servers, on your premises or in a private cloud you control. Patient data never touches our infrastructure. We set it up, we support it — but we never see what's inside.

Deployed on your server — on-premise or private cloud you control
No FirstTouch personnel ever access your patient data
