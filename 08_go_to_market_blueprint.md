# First Touch
## Go-To-Market Blueprint (EMA-First)

Last update: 2026-02-24

## 1. Strategic Objective

Build a premium B2B pipeline for workflow orchestration pilots while strengthening Jan Kluz's primary market identity as CEO of EMA AI.

Primary commercial objective:
- Book qualified discovery calls from LinkedIn and direct outreach.
- Convert calls into paid diagnostic sprints.
- Convert diagnostics into constrained pilots.

## 2. Positioning Hierarchy

Use this order everywhere:

1. EMA AI operator credibility (primary)
2. First Touch as orchestration framework (secondary)
3. AI team-leader long-term direction (strategic horizon)

## 3. ICP Prioritization

### ICP A (Priority)
- Regulated financial services operators.
- 150+ employee organizations.
- Clear leadership bottlenecks in routing/approvals/compliance operations.

### ICP B
- B2B SaaS and scaleups with high RevOps and CS workflow complexity.

### ICP C
- Legal and compliance-heavy service firms.

## 4. Buying Committee Map

- Economic buyer: CEO, COO
- Operator owner: Head of Ops, RevOps Lead, Head of CS
- Control owner: IT/Security, Compliance
- Budget influence: Finance

## 5. Core Offers

1. Workflow Bottleneck Map Session (25 minutes, free)
2. Diagnostic Sprint (2 weeks, paid)
3. Constrained Pilot (6-8 weeks, paid)
4. Scale Program (quarterly, paid)

## 6. Demand Generation Channels

Primary:
- LinkedIn founder-led content + direct outreach

Secondary:
- Warm referrals from existing ecosystem and partner network
- Targeted operator communities and events

Support:
- LP as conversion destination
- Lead magnet for list growth and qualification

## 7. GTM Engine by Stage

### Stage 1: Attention
- Publish EMA-first operator content.
- Drive to LP for call booking or lead magnet.

### Stage 2: Interest
- Use DM threads to diagnose workflow pain.
- Share specific map/checklist asset.

### Stage 3: Qualification
- Confirm quarter priority, owner, scope readiness.

### Stage 4: Conversion
- Run 25-minute working call.
- Close paid diagnostic sprint.

### Stage 5: Expansion
- Convert diagnostic to pilot.
- Expand workflow lanes in the same account.

## 8. Core KPI Targets

Top of funnel:
- Connection acceptance rate >= 30%
- Target-account engagement rate >= 8%

Middle:
- DM reply rate >= 20%
- Diagnostic-question response >= 30%

Bottom:
- Call booking from qualified DM >= 25%
- Call show rate >= 70%
- Diagnostic close rate >= 30%

## 9. Weekly Operating Cadence

- Monday: publish + target list update + connection wave
- Tuesday: comments + DM follow-up wave 1
- Wednesday: publish + connection wave 2
- Thursday: DM follow-up wave 2 + qualification
- Friday: publish + call invitations + pipeline review

## 10. Guardrails

- No side-hustle framing.
- No standalone First Touch identity push.
- No generic AI claims without workflow proof.
- No call invitation before relevance signal.

## 11. 90-Day Output Targets

- 36-48 high-signal LinkedIn posts
- 600-900 quality connection requests
- 120-180 DM conversations
- 25-40 discovery calls
- 8-15 paid diagnostics
- 3-8 constrained pilots

## 12. Decision Rules

Increase volume when:
- acceptance rate and reply rate hold.

Improve messaging before scaling when:
- acceptance drops below 25% or reply below 15%.

Tighten ICP when:
- calls book but diagnostics do not close.
