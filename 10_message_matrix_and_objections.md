## 3. Objection Handling Library

### Objection: "We already use AI tools."
Response:
- "That is a good baseline. The issue is usually not tooling, it is ownership and routing logic. We focus there first."

### Objection: "We need a full platform, not a pilot."
Response:
- "A constrained pilot protects budget and governance. It gives evidence before broad platform commitment."

### Objection: "Security/compliance will block this."
Response:
- "That is why thresholds and audit traces are designed before automation depth. Governance is built into scope, not added later."

### Objection: "We do not have time for this now."
Response:
- "Then a 25-minute map is the right start. If no active quarter priority exists, we stop immediately."

### Objection: "Can you send pricing first?"
Response:
- "Yes. Pricing depends on lane complexity and governance scope. We can provide low/mid/high anchors now and confirm exact fit after one workflow map."

### Objection: "Is this a chatbot?"
Response:
- "No. This is orchestration and policy control around workflow execution."

### Objection: "Who owns this internally?"
Response:
- "Usually COO/RevOps with IT/Security and Compliance input. We require one accountable owner before kickoff."

## 4. DM Reframing Lines
