# First Touch
## LinkedIn 30-Day Calendar (EMA-Aligned, No Split Narrative)

Last update: 2026-02-24

## Rules

- 3-4 posts per week.
- Every post must fit one lane:
  - `Business` (EMA execution),
  - `AI` (First Touch orchestration),
  - `Lifestyle`,
  - `Mindset`.
- Every post must have a brand track:
  - `EMA Core` (default),
  - `Founder POV`,
  - `First Touch Signal` (limited).
- Monthly brand ratio target:
  - EMA Core: 70-80%
  - Founder POV: 15-20%
  - First Touch Signal: 5-10%
- No side-hustle framing.
- No clickbait.

## Format template for every post

1. One-line clarity hook.
2. 3-5 short paragraphs with specific claim.
3. One implementation insight.
4. One practical CTA question.

## Brand-Track CTA Guidance

- `EMA Core` post CTA: workflow pain, operating results, implementation interest.
- `Founder POV` CTA: decision principles, execution discipline, operator conversation.
- `First Touch Signal` CTA: framework request or workflow-map asset (not direct "book demo").
